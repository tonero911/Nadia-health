HOW DO I CHOOSE THE RIGHT HEALTH INSURANCE PLAN FOR ME?

Choosing the right plan depends on your healthcare needs, budget, and preferences. Consider factors like monthly premiums, deductibles, co-pays, network of providers, and coverage for prescription drugs. It may be helpful to compare plans and consult with a health insurance advisor.

HEALTH MAINTENANCE ORGANIZATIONS (HMOS)

(HMOs) are a type of health insurance plan designed to provide comprehensive healthcare services through a network of designated providers and facilities.

PREFERRED PROVIDER ORGANIZATIONS (PPOS)

(PPOs) are a popular type of health insurance plan known for their flexibility and broad network of healthcare providers.
